Welcome

I had the immense privilege of growing up traveling the world with my parents. Overtime I began to slowly capture the beauty around. These are some of my photographs that capture my perspective of the world.

The majority of the images are of my 2017 year in Europe. Over the summer, I attended a conference in Koln, Germany(discussed in the Current Work section), and enjoyed the many museums and restaurants throughout the city. It was the first stop in a longer trip throughout Europe.
